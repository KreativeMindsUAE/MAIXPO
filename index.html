What is MAIXPO

Marketing is being
rewritten by AI.
Are you leading it?

MAIXPO 2026 is the region's dedicated AI marketing conference and expo — a two-city series covering Southeast Asia and the GCC. Two days per city of real strategies, proven tools, and honest conversations about how artificial intelligence is transforming the way brands grow.

This is not a hype fest. No vague keynotes about "the future." MAIXPO brings together working marketing professionals — CMOs, digital leads, agency founders, brand managers — who are actually deploying AI in campaigns, creative, analytics, and customer experience right now.

Whether you're just starting your AI journey or already running AI-powered campaigns, MAIXPO gives you the tools, contacts, and clarity to move faster and spend smarter.

FAQ

Common questions.
Straight answers.

Who is MAIXPO for?
Marketing managers, CMOs, digital leads, agency founders, brand strategists, ecommerce managers, and entrepreneurs who are serious about using AI to grow. If you're responsible for marketing results, MAIXPO is for you.
What's the difference between KL and Dubai editions?
KL in September is focused on the SEA marketing community. Dubai (date TBA) is adapted for the GCC. Same format, same practical approach, different local context and speaker lineup. Sponsors and exhibitors can book one or both cities.
Do I need a technical background to attend?
Not at all. MAIXPO is built for marketing practitioners, not engineers. Every session is designed for people who run campaigns, manage brands, and drive growth — no coding required.
Can I attend both cities?
Yes, and we encourage it. KL in September, Dubai (date TBA) — the full series gives you the regional network, both city audiences, and continuity across the AI marketing conversation. Series passes with a discount will be available.
What language is the event in?
All sessions, workshops, and materials are in English. MAIXPO is designed as a regional international event welcoming attendees from across Southeast Asia, the GCC, and South Asia.
Can my company sponsor or exhibit?
Yes. We offer sponsorship packages from $3,000 per city and exhibitor booths from $999 per city. A Series Partner package covers both cities in one contract. Email sponsors@maixpo.com for a full prospectus.
What is the refund policy?
Full refund available if the event does not proceed. Ticket transfers are allowed up to 14 days before the event. No refunds for cancellations within 14 days, but tickets are fully transferable.
I'm interested in speaking. How do I apply?
We welcome applications from working marketing professionals with real AI implementation experience. No vendor pitches or sales presentations. Apply at speakers@maixpo.com with your topic and brief bio.
